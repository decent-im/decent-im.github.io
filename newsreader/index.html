Newsreader service

Deutsch

A reliable web feed reader service with new features developed here.

  • Pure information: never miss new information from the people who have earned your trust, avoid distractions
  • No cookies, no dialogs, no ads, more privacy
  • Universal: most websites have feeds. Simply give it the page URL, it will detect the feed
  • Mobile-friendly: native web interface works great and is fast on mobile, also there are many mobile apps
  • Give your eyes some rest, listen on the go, with integrated modern speech synthesizer Piper on high settings.
  • More than RSS/Atom: built-in web scraping, also there are handy services providing feeds (1, 2)
  • Freedom from lock-in: FreshRSS is easy to host yourself, I will happily support you in migration

I've set it up for myself, it proved to be excellent so I share it with others.
